Experience

Roy trained as a HVAC Design Engineer for the West Midlands Regional Health Authority involved in the design of mechanical services for major healthcare projects. Throughout his career Roy has worked extensively within Building Engineering Services Design Consultancy completing projects within both public and private business sectors, specialising in healthcare work. Roy joined IBSEC in 2002 as a director, from a major consultancy practice involved primarily with healthcare and education projects.
