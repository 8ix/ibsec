Design

IBSEC have a wealth of experience in design that range from specifying the requirements of a complete building, a whole floor, a suite in a department down to a single room.

Our experience draws on knowledge and understanding of functionality, identifying key adjacencies, accommodating flows of work and people and ensuring all the environmental requirements are met.

IBSEC has experience in appraising sites, master planning, and drawing production.

We believe that the successful design of a project is produced by the development of relationships between consultant and client. IBSEC have a proven record of establishing, developing and maintaining honest, secure relationships with clients that enrich design and maximise the value for the client on every project.

IBSEC are very proud of their reputation and take enormous pleasure when asked to return and work on new projects with satisfied clients.
