Experience

In 1974 Rob trained as an Electrical Technical Apprentice at Metal Box PLC and successfully completed a 4-year indentured programmed obtaining his qualifications during that time. The following years were spent designing mainly industrial building services projects for the packaging business from R & D buildings / offices to production factory installations on 52 sites both in the UK and abroad.

In 1984 Rob moved into private practice consultancy working on blue chip company projects and government school and higher education facilities. Further experience was then gained working for a medium sized services contractor with design experience being gained in the commercial aspects of the business.

Rob then joined a small private consultancy partnership, which steadily grew over a 15-year period and became a major consultancy practice. Work during this period ranged from all aspects of Health Care projects and a wide range of Education, commercial and Industrial projects.

Relevant Experience

Healthcare - Various NHS Projects – MRI,CT scanners, X Ray, SCBU, PICU, MAU, HDU, ITU, CCU, CSSD, Radiopharmacy Operating Theatres, Renal Unit, Endoscopy Suite, Imaging, Mortuary, Adult Mental Health, Clean Rooms and General ward Refurbishments.

Education – Oxford University Bldg 304, Charles Hasting Nurse Education Centre, Keele University Undergraduate Medical centre.
